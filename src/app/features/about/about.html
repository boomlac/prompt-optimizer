Frequently Asked Questions

What is PromptOptimizer?

PromptOptimizer is a free web tool that scores AI prompts across Clarity, Completeness, Structure, Context, and Risk, then generates targeted, step-by-step improvement suggestions to maximize the quality of AI-generated output.

How does PromptOptimizer score prompts?

It runs static analysis on your prompt's structure and uses AI-powered deep analysis to identify gaps across five quality dimensions. Each dimension receives a numeric score along with specific improvement suggestions.

Who should use PromptOptimizer?

AI developers, content creators, product managers, and anyone who writes prompts for LLMs like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini who wants more consistent, high-quality output.

What are the five prompt scoring dimensions?

PromptOptimizer evaluates prompts across: Clarity (how unambiguous the instruction is), Completeness (whether all necessary context is provided), Structure (logical organization), Context (relevant background information), and Risk (potential for misinterpretation or harmful output).

Is PromptOptimizer free to use?

Yes, PromptOptimizer is completely free to use directly in your browser with no account or login required.
